Colonists use their inventories smartly when moving items, so they finish tasks with fewer round-trips and less walking back and forth — more time actually working. Plus planning tools and quality-of-life touches that build on it.

[h2]Smart inventories[/h2]
Inventories carry resources, loot etc., and efficiently unloads the contents.
This results in fewer trips to do work and hauling items.
[list]
[*] Harvested items goes into inventory without interrupting dedicated miners/farmers etc.
[*] Pick up in bulk instead of one at a time
[*] Strip corpses when hauling (configurable policies)
[*] Gather resources into inventory when constructing, cooking etc.
[*] Shared inventories — others draw from a carrier like a walking stockpile
[/list]

[h2]Smarter hauling[/h2]
What happens between picking things up and putting them away.
[list]
[*] Top up existing stacks, and let several pawns fill the same tile at once
[*] Drop a full load at storage that's already on the way
[*] Grab loose items lying along a job you're already doing
[*] Relocate supplies to storage closer to where they're used
[/list]

[h2]Bulk loading[/h2]
Anything with a manifest, loaded in bulk instead of one stack per walk.
[list]
[*] Transport pods and shuttles
[*] Map portals — loaded down [i]and[/i] hauled back up
[*] Pack animals, refuelables, and Vehicle Framework cargo
[*] Several haulers split one manifest without double-hauling
[/list]

[h2]Carrying capacity[/h2]
Pawns overload past 100% only when the slowdown beats another round-trip.
[list]
[*] A gentle curve — the heavier the load, the slower the pawn
[*] One slider from "carry freely" to "never overload"
[*] Combat Extended's weight & bulk rules take over when it's installed
[/list]

[h2]Planning tools[/h2]
Right-click → "Plan prioritized [task]…" to set up a whole job at once.
[list]
[*] Batched crafting — gather a whole batch's ingredients, then craft it all
[*] Travel-optimal routes over a patch, vein, room or growing zone, with a live preview
[*] Smart routing ends the run right next to your storage
[*] One-job construction — haul the materials and build, in a single order
[*] Pre-stock a build site, or plan a fence line for several builders at once
[/list]

[h2]Quality of life[/h2]
[list]
[*] Per-pawn control — leave a skilled miner mining while others clear the output
[*] Optional dumb-labor overrides — any pawn can haul, clean or cut plants
[*] Extensive in-game settings with per-option help, plus savable, shareable profiles
[/list]

[b]…and that's just the highlights — more than 25 improvements, each with their own toggles.[/b]

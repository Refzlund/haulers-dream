Haul After Slaughter: when a colonist finishes killing an animal, the fresh carcass is hauled straight to storage (a freezer or corpse stockpile) so it doesn't rot where it fell. Two independent toggles, both default ON — slaughtered (tamed) carcasses, which vanilla never hauls itself, and hunted (wild) carcasses, where the hunter promptly grabs its kill if a hunt was interrupted right after the killing blow (a clean hunt already self-hauls, so this never double-hauls). Only hauls when a reachable storage spot accepts the body; otherwise the carcass is left exactly as vanilla.

Harvest and haul polish, from a top-to-bottom verification of the feature: an idle pawn now scoops its pending drops and unloads in one trip instead of sometimes two; leavings from an instantly-cancelled frame can no longer be credited to a bystander deconstructing something else; pawns no longer walk to drops that were forbidden in the meantime (or left on another map); and under Combat Extended, scooped yields that merge into an existing stack are now fully registered with CE's loadout tracker, so custom-loadout pawns no longer drop part of their harvest mid-run.

**Consumer-aware storage routing (opt-in, off by default).** Ported from "While You're Up"'s "haul before carry": before a pawn carries a resource to a build site or crafting bill, it can relocate the largest nearby stack of that material to storage *closer to the consuming job*, so future fetches are short — plus optional same-/equal-priority relocation. Four sub-toggles (supplies, ingredients, equal-priority, stockpiles). It's carefully guarded so it never double-acts with Hauler's Dream's own build-from-inventory / batch-craft / bulk systems, and it stands down rather than risk a double-haul. Enable it in **En-route & Routing** in settings.

**Smarter unload ordering: nearest destination first (on by default).** When a pawn makes its unload trip carrying several different items, it now empties the nearest storage destination fully before walking to the next, instead of going in item-category order — less zig-zagging across the base. Items with nowhere to go are never stranded (they're just visited last, exactly as before). Ported from "While You're Up"'s efficient-unloading, re-expressed on Hauler's Dream's own unload. Toggle in settings.

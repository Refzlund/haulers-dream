See replies and fixes to your reports right on the main menu.

When the developer replies to one of your in-game reports, marks it solved, or ships a fix in an update, a small notification now appears in the bottom-right of the main menu. Click it to read the reply or comment back without leaving the game, or dismiss it with the x. It only shows things you have not seen yet, and only for reports you submitted. A "Main menu notifications" setting on the Advanced tab lets you choose how much shows up, from every event down to only fixes (with a heads-up when a newer version is available), or turn it off entirely.

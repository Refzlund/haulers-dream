Add an "Only on maps you control" option under "Also work on caravan / non-home maps". When Hauler's Dream is working off your home colony, you can now limit it to maps you actually control (your home, a temporary camp or settled site, and any map where you have built storage) so it stands down on maps you are only passing through or attacking, such as ambush sites and enemy bases. Off by default, preserving the current behavior of working on all non-home maps.

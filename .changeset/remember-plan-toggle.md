Reuse your last route plan in one click.

A new "Remember plan" toggle sits with the other interface toggles in the bottom-right corner. Turn it on and choosing "Plan prioritized …" reuses the settings you last set for that kind of target right away, without opening the planner window; the right-click option reads "(remembered)" so you know it will. Turn it off and the planner opens each time, as before. Hovering the option highlights the toggle so it is easy to find, and each kind of target remembers its own settings.
